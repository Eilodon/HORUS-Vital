# Legal Disclaimer

**HORUS-Vital is a research and hackathon prototype. It is NOT a medical device,
clinical diagnostic tool, or substitute for professional medical advice.**

- Heart rate, HRV, respiration, and other signals are estimated using
  remote photoplethysmography (rPPG) from a consumer camera. Accuracy is
  significantly lower than clinical-grade equipment.
- MedPsy-1.7B is a quantized language model. Its outputs are not medically
  validated and may be incorrect, incomplete, or misleading.
- Do not use this application to make health, clinical, or emergency decisions.
- In a medical emergency, contact emergency services immediately.

By using this application you acknowledge that you understand and accept
